About EduConnect

EduConnect is a comprehensive hybrid digital attendance and engagement platform designed to modernize how schools track student participation. By replacing traditional paper-based methods with automated QR scanning and real-time data synchronization, EduConnect eliminates inefficiencies, reduces clerical workload, and provides reliable digital records that support transparency and accountability.

Our system addresses the common challenges schools face: lost or damaged attendance sheets, delays in recording, difficulty tracking participation, and lack of real-time data. With EduConnect, administrators can make informed decisions quickly, students can check their attendance records easily, and the entire academic community benefits from a more organized and accessible attendance management system.
